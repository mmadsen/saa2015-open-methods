Tools for Transparency and Replicability of Simulation in Archaeology

 

Mark E. Madsen and Carl P. Lipo

University of Washington, Seattle and California State University at Long Beach

Challenges

  • Validation and correctness
  • Reuse
  • Data scale and model complexity

Validation and correctness

  • Investigators: is my model working as expected?
  • Editors and reviewers: does this research represent solid research?
  • Readers: can I trust the results?

Reuse

  • Not just about other researchers
  • Reinventing the wheel is common
  • Even by one person across projects

Data scale and model complexity

  • Large parameter spaces = Large amounts of data
  • Many problems require a pipeline of analyses
  • Tracking data through multiple analyses is difficult
